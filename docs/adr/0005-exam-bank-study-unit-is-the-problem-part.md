# Exam Bank Study Unit Is The Problem Part

Exam Bank progress and revision are centered on `ExamProblemPart`, not only the whole `ExamProblem`, because Bac problems are usually split into several parts and each part can have its own video correction, written énoncé, formulas, labs, and revision status. The whole problem remains the official Bac container, while the part capsule is the student-facing study unit.
