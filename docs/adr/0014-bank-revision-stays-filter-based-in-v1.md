# Bank Revision Stays Filter-Based In V1

Exercise Workspace and Exam Bank revision should stay inside their own bank filters in v1. Students can filter by states such as `again`, `partial`, `mastered`, saved, and retry-later, but the product does not build a unified Revision Queue yet.

A suggested unified revision queue can be considered later after the banks have enough usage data. Deferring it keeps v1 focused on the bank workspaces, avoids mixing self-reported exercise/exam signals with verified quiz mistakes, and leaves the Mistake Notebook centered on official verified quiz attempts.
